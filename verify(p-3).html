Georgette, a lightweight, sheer fabric with a crinkled texture, is a popular choice for sarees due to its fluidity and draping qualities. Originating from the French word "Georgette de Soie," meaning "silk georgette," this fabric is commonly made from silk, synthetic fibers, or a blend of both. The unique weaving process creates a fabric that is both soft to the touch and visually appealing.
