As a local artisan, I take great pride in crafting these exquisite pieces using traditional techniques passed down through generations. These carpets are made in a "karkhana" or "carpet factory" where skilled weavers like myself work together to create wonders of art. We use only the highest quality materials, including pure silk, pure Merino wool, and a perfect blend of wool and silk, ensuring that our carpets are not only beautiful but also durable. No two carpets are alike. you are investing in a piece of art that tells a story. By supporting local artisans like myself and choosing our handcrafted carpets, you are helping to preserve an endangered craft that has been a source of pride for Kashmir for centuries. As the manufacturer of Kashmiri carpets, we are always grateful to hear positive feedback from our customers. Your purchase not only brings beauty into your home but also contributes to the sustainability of our community. Thank you for supporting local artisans and choosing our unique and exquisite carpets.
